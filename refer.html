How attribution works

When someone visits GeniusSeeker with your link, a first-touch attribution cookie is saved on their device for 90 days. If they go on to sign up for a sales team on the platform within that window, you're credited.

No pixel tracking. No email capture here. Just a single query parameter and local storage — transparent by design.

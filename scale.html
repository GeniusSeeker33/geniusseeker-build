What you get on your subdomain

Branded login, isolated data, configurable KPIs and scenarios, your own compensation rules, and optional SSO with your identity provider. Onboarding, training, and comp reporting all live under one URL for your team.

Why per-tenant subdomains

Isolation

Each tenant's data, scenarios, and comp rules live in their own silo. Your reps never see another company's accounts, and vice versa.

Configurability

KPI thresholds, progression levels, and comp tiers are all configured per tenant. The platform stays the same; the meaning bends to your business.

Branding

Your logo, colors, and app name. Reps see your brand first, the platform second — so adoption feels like an internal tool, not another vendor login.

The network effect

Reps grow with the network

As more tenants join, more scenarios get authored. Your reps train against a growing library of real-world objections — without exposing your data.

Benchmarks get real

Anonymized readiness benchmarks across tenants let you see how your team compares — useful for enablement planning without anyone's private comp showing up.

Referral flywheel

Sales pros who refer into one tenant earn residuals. As more companies join, the referrer network compounds — which is how the whole loop closes.

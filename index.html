One network, three businesses, same flywheel

The reason this works is that each product feeds the next. More credentialed candidates make Talent more useful. Better-matched hires make Sales teams ramp faster. Stronger sales teams create more demand to hire again.

01 → Path

Candidates prove they're ready

Short, skill-based assessments and real-world scenarios replace the old "tell me about yourself" loop. Candidates build a verified portfolio as they explore.

02 → Talent

Employers hire based on readiness

Companies search credentials and scenario scores, not keywords. Interviews start higher up the funnel because proof comes first.

03 → Perform

New hires ramp and keep growing

Once hired, reps keep training on the same platform through real scenarios. Managers see readiness, not completion checkboxes. That data loops back to make hiring sharper.

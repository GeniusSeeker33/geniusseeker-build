Why We Built GeniusSeeker

Hiring is broken — and not just for candidates.

Talented people are passed over because of bias, not ability. Companies post jobs with hidden salary ranges and wonder why trust erodes. Recruiters chase invoices instead of building relationships. And nobody has a verifiable record of whether any of it was fair.

GeniusSeeker was built to change that. Every employer commitment — salary ranges, diverse panels, structured feedback — is attested and verified over time through real candidate reviews. Every candidate builds an on-chain identity that belongs to them, not to a resume database someone else owns.

We believe work should feel safe, transparent, and fair for everyone involved. That's not a tagline. It's the architecture.

How GeniusSeeker Works

Simple. Transparent. Documented.

1. Employer Gets Verified

Companies apply and attest to salary transparency, DEI practices, and hiring standards. Desiree reviews every application personally.

2. Candidate Builds Identity

STEAM professionals create verified profiles anchored to their Hedera identity — their data, their record, owned by them.

3. Roles Are Posted

Verified employers post roles with salary ranges. Recruiters share aligned openings and earn commission on successful placements.

4. Offer & Contract

Employers send structured offers. Candidates review, counter, or accept — with every step recorded and transparent.

5. Work Happens

Work continues with your existing tools. GeniusSeeker tracks milestones and keeps the record clean.

6. Reviews Build the Record

After every engagement, candidates review the employer. That's the third-party verification that makes GeniusSeeker data trustworthy.

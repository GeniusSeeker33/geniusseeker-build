Why this helps you

You're judged on what you can do

Keyword-matching applicant systems are why good people get filtered out. Here, your credentials and scenarios are the filter — in your favor.

You can switch paths without starting over

Skills carry. A credential in one area counts when you pivot into a related one. Your history doesn't reset every time you change direction.

You keep learning after you're hired

If the company you join uses GeniusSeeker internally, your training continues on the same platform. You're building one record over time, not scattered pieces.

Frequently asked

Is it really free?

Yes. Candidates don't pay. Companies pay to hire from the network, which is how the platform funds itself.

Do I need a resume?

No. You can start with a quiz. You build your profile as you go.

Who sees my results?

Only you — until you opt in to be visible to hiring teams. You can turn that off at any time.

Will companies actually contact me?

The candidates who complete credentials and stay active get seen first. The network is still growing — early movers benefit most.
